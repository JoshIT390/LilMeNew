Title: Curious George Goes Camping

Author: H. A. Rey

Summary:
A man brings a curious monkey out of the jungle and into civilization and the monkey and the man explore the new worlds and consequences created from those decisions.

Lessons Learned:
1. It is not inherently bad to be curious. It is natural and normal to be curious.

2. Curiosity comes from a desire to find answers to questions and a want to learn.

3. Sometimes when you try to do things beyond your aptitudes, you will fall, and others will be needed to rescue you. (Curious George trying to fly like a bird).

Continue the Learning!:
 1. Ask your child what other moral, humorous, or innovative ideas were inside the story?

2. The often heard phrase "Curiosity killed the cat" is challenged in this story. How can you phrase that curiosity is not a bad thing?
